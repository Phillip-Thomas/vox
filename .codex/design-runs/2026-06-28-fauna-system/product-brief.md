# Product Brief

## Page Goal

Add a performant procedural fauna renderer that makes inhabited planets feel more alive and cohesive with the recent grass, flora, wind, and voxel-effect upgrades.

## Primary Action

The player should notice sparse animated critters in suitable surface biomes without the fauna blocking movement, overwhelming terrain readability, or tanking frame rate.

## User Motivation

The world is moving toward a progression of rendering transcendence. Fauna needs to share the same environmental systems so animals, plants, and voxel phenomena feel like one living planet.

## Success Proxy

- `?effects=fauna` test harness shows multiple animal archetypes on grass, dirt, and sand patches.
- Animals are visibly animated and wind-influenced.
- Fauna density and distance are controlled by graphics quality.
- Typecheck, focused fauna tests, and full verify pass.

## Language / Claim Constraints

- No in-game explanatory copy.
- Harness copy can be functional and dev-facing.
