Lessons learned:
- Do not clamp HDR color grading before the final ACES tone map; it creates brittle highlight behavior and can make shaders feel disconnected.
- For this game, the premium/cinematic improvement comes more from edge/contrast restraint than from a heavy painterly filter.
- Keep older laptop paths composer-free by default; tune HIGH/ULTRA separately from MEDIUM/LOW.
- Deterministic agent vantages are better than random spawn screenshots for render-style comparisons.
