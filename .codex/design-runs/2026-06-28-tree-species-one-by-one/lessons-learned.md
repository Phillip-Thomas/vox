Lessons:
- Do not use an inward foliage threshold for visible trees. It makes leaves look like they are attached to stems even when the branch skeleton is correct.
- Candidate selection needs species ownership. A single "tips and outer order" rule hid weak cases; conical, umbrella, weeping, wispy, frond, and round need separate rules.
- Dense conical trees need growth hardening, not just more leaf cards. High apical dominance can starve side branches unless capped in the generator.
- Broadleaf top stumps should be solved in trunk geometry, not by adding top leaf disks; top disks read detached in the harness.
- Keep `tree-test.html` captureable with stable `?only=` species URLs. The variety board is essential because a closeup seed can pass while another planet seed stays sparse.
