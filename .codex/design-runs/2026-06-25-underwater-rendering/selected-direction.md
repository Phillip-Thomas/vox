# Selected Direction

Selected direction: source-gated cinematic shafts.

Implementation thesis:

- Underwater light shafts should be sourced by the surface/sun aperture, not by arbitrary bright terrain pixels.
- The medium should do more visual work through haze and cool wash, so close terrain remains readable without overpowering the frame.

Rejected direction:

- Full godray disablement. It would remove the artifact, but it would also remove the desired sun-through-water effect.

Taste assumption:

- A slightly stylized teal underwater volume fits Paravoxia better than a literal dark scuba simulation.
