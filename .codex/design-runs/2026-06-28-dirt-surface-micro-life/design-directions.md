# Design Directions

## Direction A: Loose Topsoil With Hidden Micro-Crawlers

Thesis: dirt reads as an uneven living skin only when the camera comes close. Use flattened crumb/fleck geometry plus tiny worm-like dark ribbons that subtly ripple along the voxel face.

Why it fits: it answers the user request directly, keeps gameplay view quiet, and shares the spawned surface-effect architecture.

## Direction B: Visible Burrows And Critter Trails

Thesis: dirt gets larger burrow arcs, little trail marks, and more obvious worm/crawler silhouettes.

Why rejected: stronger readability would make dirt compete with grass/trees and turn the request into an obvious creature layer instead of a close-inspection soil detail.
