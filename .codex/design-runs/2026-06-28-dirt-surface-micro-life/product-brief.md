# Product Brief

User job: inspect terrain closely and feel that each material has its own living/rendered surface language.

Primary action: view a dirt patch in the voxel effect harness and recognize loose topsoil with tiny near-hidden movement.

Success proxy: dirt has comparable fidelity to sand dust, grass, and trees while staying cohesive with the planet wind system.

Language constraints: visible harness labels remain descriptive and concise.

Required states: desktop harness, mobile harness, quality-gated runtime behavior, no spawned effect on unsupported materials.
