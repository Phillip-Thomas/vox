# Selected Direction

Selected: Direction A, loose topsoil with hidden micro-crawlers.

Taste assumptions:
- The user wants organic richness, not obvious collectible/resource creatures.
- Dirt should remain visually quieter than grass and sand dust from normal gameplay distance.
- Close inspection should reward the player with small movement and uneven topsoil detail.

Rejected:
- Direction B because it risks making the dirt read noisy or cartoony.

Human review note:
- Review crawler visibility after screenshots. If it is too subtle, increase density first before increasing contrast.
