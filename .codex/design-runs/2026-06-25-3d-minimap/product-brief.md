# Product Brief

## Page Goal

Make co-op exploration and base-building feel more spatially connected by adding a beautiful, glanceable 3D minimap to the live HUD.

## Primary Action

Read orientation and teammate/object positions without pausing or opening the star map.

## User Motivation

Players want to know where their friends, ship, campfires, and base pieces are while moving, flying, or building.

## Success Proxy

- The minimap is visible during gameplay and unmistakably 3D.
- The player's heading updates smoothly.
- Remote players and persistent placed objects appear as distinct pips.
- The instrument does not interrupt controls or clutter the critical center view.

## Language Constraints

- Use short scanner labels only.
- Avoid instructional text inside the HUD.
- Keep all text compact enough for mobile.
