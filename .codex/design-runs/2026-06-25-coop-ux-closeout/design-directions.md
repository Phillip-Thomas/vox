# Design Directions

## Direction A - Compact Crew Manifest

Add a small roster section inside the existing Co-op panel and enrich the in-game badge with a concise player count. Rows show current player identity, connected state, and owner/local labels.

Why it fits: it solves the trust problem without changing the product's current HUD density.

## Direction B - Full Room Console

Expand the Co-op panel into a broader room management console with member cards, connection diagnostics, and host controls.

Why rejected: host controls and moderation are not implemented yet, and a large social console would be premature for Phase 1 alpha.

## Direction C - HUD-Only Minimalism

Only alter the in-game badge and leave the landing panel mostly unchanged.

Why rejected: invite-room confidence matters before entering play, especially for manual two-computer testing.
