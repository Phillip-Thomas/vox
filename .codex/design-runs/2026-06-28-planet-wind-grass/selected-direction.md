# Selected Direction

Selected: Direction B, Shared Planet Wind Profile.

## Why

It satisfies the immediate grass request and establishes a clean planet-dynamic seam for future consumers. It stays pure/deterministic, avoids runtime state churn, and keeps grass as the first consumer.

## Rejected

- Direction A: too narrow; does not honor the requested future-consumer model.
- Direction C: too broad for this visual pass.

## Taste Assumptions

- "Various directions in various places" means coherent gust cells and local veer, not chaotic random jitter.
- Wind should make grass feel alive while preserving the readable voxel/alien style.
