# Selected Direction

Selected: Direction A, Micro-Cluster Hair Field.

## Why

It directly addresses the user's complaint: thinness and density are handled by the same deterministic blade instance system already in production. It avoids introducing a second renderer or texture pipeline, and it keeps per-quality control intact.

## Rejected

- Direction B: too likely to flatten the game world's living meadow feel.
- Direction C: promising, but too much architecture and performance risk for this pass.

## Taste Assumptions

- "Hair" means many fine visible strands with a soft wind shimmer, not a photorealistic fur shader.
- The grass should remain stylized enough to belong to the existing alien voxel world.
