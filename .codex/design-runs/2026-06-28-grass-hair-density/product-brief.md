# Product Brief

## Page Goal

Make grass-covered blocks look like they have many fine hairs rather than a handful of large cartoon blades.

## User Motivation

The player should believe grass is a living surface material on the planet, not a sparse decoration attached to each block.

## Primary Action

No UI action. The primary product outcome is visual: when the camera approaches or stands near a grass block, the surface reads as denser, thinner, softer vegetation.

## Success Proxy

- A grass voxel shows a visibly higher number of fine strands at gameplay distance.
- From the stored approach vantage, the field looks less grid-like and less clumped.
- No major frame/render instability or blank scene.
- Existing test suite remains healthy.

## Language And Claims

No visible product copy is introduced.

## Scoped Out

- New terrain materials.
- New grass textures.
- Changing trees, forage, water, HUD, or avatar visuals.
- Production deploy.
