# Selected Direction

Selected direction: EVA Beacon Stack.

## Rationale

This gives remote avatars a stronger launch-quality read while respecting the existing architecture. The body still carries posture and color, accessories still show action, and a small beacon/footing layer adds redundant non-color cues.

## Taste Assumptions

- Paravoxia should feel like a utilitarian sci-fi survival suit, not a cartoon party avatar.
- The readable signal should live in-world, not as another HUD panel.
- Small code-native geometry is preferable to adding model assets for this pass.

Review whether the beacon feels too "game UI" versus diegetic suit telemetry once screenshots are available.
