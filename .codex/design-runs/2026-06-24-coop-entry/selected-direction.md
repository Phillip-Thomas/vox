# Selected Direction

Selected: Radio Link Panel.

Rejected: Split Launch Choice.

## Why This Wins

The co-op path is functional infrastructure but not yet final gameplay sync. A compact radio-link panel is honest about the alpha state, preserves offline Play as the dominant path, and reuses the existing menu system.

## Taste Assumptions

- Phillip prefers useful, engineered UI over decorative marketing surfaces.
- The alpha state should feel purposeful and not apologetic.
- Compact cockpit-style controls fit Paravoxia better than large onboarding cards.

## Human Review Later

When command/snapshot integration is playable, revisit whether Co-op should become a first-class launch lane beside Offline.
