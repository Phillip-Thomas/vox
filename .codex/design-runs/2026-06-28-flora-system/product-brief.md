# Product Brief

## Goal

Add a reusable procedural flora renderer so planets can grow visible non-tree plants beyond grass: flowers, fan plants, dry seedheads, cacti/succulents, and shrubs.

## User Motivation

The player should feel that each planet has a richer ecology and that rendering quality can later evolve with the story's dimension/reality mechanics.

## Success Proxy

- The flora layer is visible in an isolated voxel harness.
- Species selection changes by material and biome climate.
- Wind motion is cohesive with grass and trees.
- Reality stage / organic intensity can turn the system down or off.
- Typecheck, tests, build, and rendered screenshots pass.

## Language Constraints

No new in-game copy is required. Harness labels can remain concise and diagnostic.
